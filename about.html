Shop

Partea is a cafe shop loacated at 453blg. Makata St. Brgy. Juanito, City of San Alfonso, Kabiya. This is also known as the "musicafe" of san Alfonso. It is well-known for it's popularity and uniqueness due to the cafe twist made by the owner. It is the one and only cafe in the province that serve live music band for the costumers. The owner made this because she wants her customers to have a relaxing and stress-free time staying at her cafe. The owner came up with a "Party" and "Tea", party because in her cafe you'll be feeling a relaxing party because of the activities and the live band. Many people have visited the cafe and always coming back because of its enjoy moments. Partea also offers different merchandise and souvenirs for all of you.
